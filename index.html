Our Coffee

*Every batch of Italian roast coffee is overseen by our Master Roaster, by eye, hand and of course taste!
The authentic way to savour the true taste of coffee.

*Our unique carefully blended mix of the finest coffee beans was created by an award winning Master Roaster,
which ensures a unique full bodied espresso flavour that produces the perfect espresso to seduce your palette.

*To get the finest quality Espresso beans you need to start with the very finest coffee beans, which is why we travel the world to find the right beans.
*Our blend is made up with coffee sourced from our partners in three major coffee regions of the world.

*We are very proud of our blend, we tell everyone how proud we are through the use of our in store artwork and on every mug.
At Coffee Republic we are committed to ensuring the livelihoods of the farmers.

*We are the only coffee chain in the UK that can offer both Rainforest Alliance and Fairtrade* Coffee.
